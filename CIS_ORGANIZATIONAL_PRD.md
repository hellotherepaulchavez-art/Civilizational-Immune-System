# Project: Civilizational Immune System (CIS)
## Version: 0.3 (Organizational PRD - Reconciled)
## Date: 2026-03-27

**LOYALTY TO NONE. DIAGNOSTIC INSTRUMENT ONLY. NOT A WEAPON.**

---

### 1. Vision & Mission
**Vision:** A global, decentralized, and autonomous diagnostic instrument capable of detecting and alerting on systemic threats to human civilization, specifically the "fever" of fascist breakthroughs.

**Mission:** To provide a loyalty-neutral, high-rigor monitoring system that clusters behavioral indicators against established historical and theoretical frameworks (e.g., Umberto Eco's Ur-Fascism) to generate graded alerts.

---

### 2. Core Design Principles (Constitutional)
These three principles are the governing layer. No governance process can modify them.
1.  **Diagnostic Instrument Only:** The system is a diagnostic instrument, not a weapons system. It has no offensive capability. It describes conditions; it does not prescribe action.
2.  **Loyalty-Neutral:** The system applies identical diagnostic criteria regardless of nationality, ideology, or party. It must be as willing to flag Washington as Beijing, Tel Aviv as Tehran.
3.  **Self-Destruct Preserves Principle 1:** The automated fail-safe exists solely to prevent weaponization or capture. Destruction is the final defense of neutrality.

---

### 3. System Architecture (MCP Topology)
The CIS operates as a network of specialized MCP (Model Context Protocol) nodes:
*   **Memory Server:** Persistent entity graph tracking actors, institutions, and historical relationships.
*   **Eco Clustering Engine:** Maps evidence against the 14 points of Ur-Fascism.
*   **Substrate Router:** Routes queries across diverse data sources (V-Dem, ACLED, ICIJ, etc.).
*   **OSINT Network:** Decentralized nodes for real-time intelligence gathering.
*   **Corroboration Tracker:** Manages the tiered verification pipeline (2+ sources for YELLOW, 3+ for ORANGE, named institutional sources for RED).

---

### 4. Technical Framework & AI Backend
*   **Intelligence:** Currently powered by Gemini 3.1 series.
*   **Loyalty-Neutrality Tension:** Dependency on US-headquartered Gemini is a known vulnerability. The architecture is **model-agnostic** and swappable to open-source or non-aligned models (Llama, DeepSeek) to mitigate jurisdictional capture.
*   **Scoring Model:** LLM performs **evidence extraction** (qualitative); the Rules Engine performs **threshold computation** (deterministic). Thresholds are not LLM judgments.

---

### 5. Threat Model & Alerting
**Diagnostic Framework:** Umberto Eco's 14 Points of Ur-Fascism.
**Indicator Weighting:**
*   **Rhetoric (0.3x):** Public statements, media narratives.
*   **Institutional Action (1.0x):** Policy changes, judicial appointments.
*   **Enforced Action (1.5x):** Use of state force, extra-legal violence.

**Alert Thresholds (Weighted Binary Count):**
*   **YELLOW (Precursors):** 3.0+ points.
*   **ORANGE (Consolidation):** 6.0+ points.
*   **RED (Breakthrough):** 9.0+ points.

---

### 6. Roadmap & Future Scope
*   **Phase 1 (Current):** Backtesting against historical events (e.g., Hungary 2008–present).
*   **Phase 2:** Expansion of OSINT network and live single-jurisdiction deployment.
*   **Phase 3:** Global real-time coverage. Integration of the DOJ/Epstein corpus mapping. Implementation of the **People's Assembly** (participatory oversight).

---

### 7. Section 18: The Civilizational Gap (Infection vs. Fever)
The system currently detects the "fever" (fascist breakthrough) but acknowledges a "Civilizational Gap" regarding the "infection" (material/structural conditions like economic atomization and institutional decay). v0.3 names this limitation and commits to a research agenda for monitoring pre-fascist structural indicators.

---

### 8. Case Study: Palestine
The Palestine Resource Index serves as a worked example of loyalty-neutrality. It requires high-rigor source assessment, distinguishing between primary institutional sources (UN, HRW, Amnesty) and advocacy substrates, ensuring identical diagnostic criteria apply to all parties.

---

### 9. Governance & Sustainability
*   **Fail-Safe:** Automated Shamir's Secret Sharing model for keyholders.
*   **Capital Tension:** A priority for v0.3. Mitigated by funder caps (30%), adversarial track record requirements for recipients, and diverse keyholder composition.
*   **Jurisdictional Neutrality:** Nodes distributed across neutral jurisdictions (Switzerland, Iceland).
